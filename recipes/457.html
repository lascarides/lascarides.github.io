Recipes » Main Course Spinach and ricotta shells with tomato and breadcrumbs

Ingredients

300 g pasta shells large
400 g spinach trimmed
400 g ricotta cheese
1 courgette grated
1/4 cup pine nuts lightly toasted
1 tsp lemon finely grated zest
2.5 cups tomato passata
1/4 cup breadcrumbs fresh, white
1.5 Tbsp parmesan cheese grated

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Cook pasta following packet directions. Drain well.
  2. Step 2 Place spinach in a large heatproof bowl. Just cover with boiling water and set aside for 30 seconds. Drain then squeeze out excess water. In a large bowl, combine chopped spinach, ricotta, courgette, pine nuts and zest. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Step 3 Preheat oven to 180ºC. Lightly oil a 1.5L/6 cup-capacity ovenproof baking dish. Spread 1 cup passata over base of dish. Spoon ricotta filling into pasta shells and arrange in a single layer in dish. Pour remaining sauce evenly over pasta then sprinkle with breadcrumbs and parmesan. Place in oven and bake for 20 minutes, or until breadcrumbs are golden.
