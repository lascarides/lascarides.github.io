Recipes » Side Dishes Sauteed kale with balsamic reduction

Ingredients

2 Tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic thinly sliced
2 cups kale rinsed and chopped
1/2 cup chicken broth
salt and pepper
2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in a skillet.
  2. Add the sliced garlic and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the kale and stir. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil, then turn down the heat to low and simmer, covered, for 5 minutes.
  5. Remove the lid and add the balsamic vinegar. Bring to a boil, then turn the heat to low and simmer uncovered until all of the liquid is gone. Stir occasionally.
