Recipes » Main Course Chicken Pie (from Mum)

Ingredients

chicken cooked
1 onion
1 leek
1 spring onion
1 carrot
1 stalk celery
butter
flour
milk
salt and pepper
cheese
3 sheets puff pastry

Instructions

  1. For chicken pie, usually have an onion or leek or spring onion, a carrot, sometimes a celery stick. Chop these up and sauté to soften in butter or oil, remove. Add more butter/oil and brown cut up chicken pieces. I have been known to microwave the chicken but this can toughen it. Or use left overs from roast chicken if you have them. You can also add any leftover cooked potatoes, pumpkin.
  2. Remove chicken from pan, add more butter, a bit of flour and make a thick white sauce with milk or milk powder and water. Season with pepper and minimal salt, can add a bit of cheese if you like. Put cooked veg and chicken in pie plate, pour over white sauce. Top with shortcrust or puff pastry, bake at 180C until pastry is cooked.
