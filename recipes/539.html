Recipes » Main Course Chirashizushi

Ingredients

2 cups sushi rice cooked
3 tsp rice vinegar
2 Tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt
2 carrot diced
100 g shiitake mushrooms dried (about 3), soaked in 300ml warm water, sliced, liquid reserved
1 tsp dashi powder
2 tsp mirin
2 tsp sake
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1/2 tsp sugar
2 eggs
1 handful green beans blanched, and sliced on the diagonal
1 packet salmon roe

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, add the rice vinegar, sugar and salt; mix well – this is sushi vinegar mix.
  2. While the rice cooks, place the carrots, shiitake, dashi, mirin, sake, soy, sugar and reserved mushroom stock in a medium-sized pot. Bring to boil and simmer for 5-8 minutes. Turn off heat; set aside to cool.
  3. Crack the eggs in a small bowl and beat well. Place a small frying pan over medium heat and add 1 teaspoon oil. Pour ¼ to ½ of the egg mix into the pan to make a thin omelette. Repeat. Cut into julienne.
  4. When the rice is cooked, place in a large non-stainless steel bowl. Pour the sushi vinegar mix over and mix well.
  5. Drain the liquid from the carrots and shiitake; mix the vegetables with the rice.
  6. To assemble, divide rice between plates. Sprinkle omelette strips on top.
  7. Garnish with green beans and salmon roe if using.

Prep time • Cook time

Chirashi sushi is a favourite for occasions such as weddings, birthdays and festivals.
