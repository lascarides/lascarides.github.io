Recipes » Main Course North African squash and chickpea stew

Ingredients

2 tsp canola oil
2 onions large, diced
2 cloves garlic finely chopped
1 cup celery finely diced
1 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger ground
sea salt
black pepper
100 g red lentils
1 can chickpeas rinsed and drained
5 g saffron
500 ml tomato sauce roasted
1 handful parsley roughly chopped
1 handful cilantro roughly chopped
300 g squash or pumpkin
1.2 L vegetable stock
1 bay leaf
60 g orzo or other small pasta
1 handful dates to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onions and saute until just starting to turn golden. Turn down the heat to medium-low and add the garlic, celery, turmeric, cinnamon, ginger, and 1 teaspoon pepper. Saute for a couple of minutes.
  2. Now add the lentils, chickpeas, saffron, tomato sauce, parsley, and about half the cilantro. Cook over low heat for 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, peel and seed the squash and cut into large cubes. Add to the pan with the stock and bay leaf. Cover and simmer gently for about 30 minutes. Add the pasta and simmer until it is cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately, scattered with the remaning cilantro leaves and with a few dates on the side, if you like.
