Recipes » Snacks and Sandwiches Homemade muesli bars

Ingredients

oil spray
1 cup oats wholegrain
1/2 cup puffed rice cereal
1 cup sultanas or other mixed dried fruit such as apricots, prunes, dates
2 Tbsp sunflower seeds
2 Tbsp sesame seeds
4 Tbsp wheat bran
2 Tbsp self-raising flour
1 egg
1 egg white
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp apple juice concentrate

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 180°C. Spray a 20cm x 30cm cake tin with oil and line with baking paper. Set aside. Place all dry ingredients into a large bowl and mix well.
  2. Step 2 In another bowl whisk together egg and egg white until frothy. Make a well in the centre of the dry mixture and pour in eggs, oil and juice concentrate. Mix until all ingredients are coated.
  3. Step 3 Press mixture into prepared tin. Cook for about 25 minutes until golden brown.
