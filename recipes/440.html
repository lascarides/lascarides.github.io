Recipes » Main Course Beans and rice

Ingredients

2 Tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic crushed
1/2 tsp cumin ground
1.5 cups rice uncooked
400 g tomatoes diced
400 g kidney beans drained and rinsed
1 tsp stock powder heaped, dissolved in 1 cup water
water
4 Tbsp cheese freshly grated

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Heat oil in a large saucepan and gently cook garlic and cumin for a couple of minutes. Add rice to pan, mixing to coat well with garlic and cook for a further minute.
  2. Step 2 Stir in tomatoes, kidney beans and stock. Cover and cook on a low heat until rice is cooked and a little creamy. Check the pan regularly during this time and stir in half a cup of water each time – this keeps the rice from drying out and sticking to the pan (you may need to do this 4 or 5 times).
  3. Step 3 Once rice is cooked through, serve with a light sprinkling of grated cheese. Enjoy with salad or vegetables.
