Recipes » Main Course Devilled Sausages

Ingredients

8 sausages beef or pork
2 onion
2 apple granny smith
1 Tbsp olive oil plus 1 tblsp butter
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 Tbsp soy sauce
salt and pepper
1 Tbsp chutney i use homemade feijoa chutney but any good chutney will work well
1.5 tsp sweet chilli sauce
1 cup beef stock
1 tsp cornflour mixed with a little cold water, (if needed)

Instructions

  1. Brown sausages, slice into chunks diagonally and place in casserole dish.
  2. Slice onions and peel & slice apples and saute in the oil & butter, till softened slightly.
  3. Add the brown sugar and allow to gently caramelise a little.
  4. Add rest of ingredients, mix and pour over sausages.
  5. Cover and cook 45-50 mins at 180 degrees C.
  6. If desired thicken juices with cornflour and water.
  7. Serve with fluffy mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables.
  8. Superb winter comfort food!
