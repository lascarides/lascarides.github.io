Recipes » Main Course Laksa with Turmeric, Kale and Fish

Ingredients

2 cups vegetable stock
1 Tbsp tamari
1 Tbsp fish sauce
1 Tbsp ginger grated
1 tsbp turmeric fresh, grated
1/2 tsp honey
4 carrots small, chopped
4 leaves kale shredded
100 g mushrooms diced
250 g fish white, cut into bite-size pieces
1/2 can coconut milk
150 g rice noodles
2 tsp raw sugar
1 pinch chili flakes

Instructions

  1. In medium-large pot, combine stock, tamari, ginger, turmeric, honey, and fish sauce.
  2. Bring to boil and add carrots. Simmer 8-10 minutes, or until tender.
  3. Add fish, mushrooms, kale, coconut milk, and noodles and stir. Add sugar and chili flakes.
  4. Bring back to a simmer and cook for a further 8 minutes or until the rice noodles are soft.
  5. Season as needed and serve.
