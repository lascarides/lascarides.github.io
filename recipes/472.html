Recipes » Main Course Cowboy casserole

Ingredients

1 cup macaroni pasta
500 g beef mince lean (or 50:50 beef/pork)
1/2 onion chopped
800 g baked beans 2 x 420g cans
1/2 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp cumin ground
1/4 tsp chilli powder
pinches oregano dried
1 Tbsp tomato paste heaped
1 cup cheddar cheese grated
courgette

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 180°C. Cook macaroni following packet directions.
  2. Step 2 Meanwhile, heat a medium-sized saucepan. Add meat and stir until browned. Add onion and keep stirring until softened.
  3. Step 3 Mix in baked beans, sauce, spices and tomato paste then sprinkle in stock powder. Mix well then add cooked macaroni.
  4. Step 4 Pour mixture into an ovenproof casserole-type dish and cover with grated cheese. Heat in oven for about until cheese is melted and bubbling.
