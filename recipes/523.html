Recipes » Main Course Chicken Burritos

Ingredients

2 cups rice
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 onion chopped
2 cloves garlic smashed and minced
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
250 g chicken breast
1 can chilli beans
1 can corn kernels small
1 bunch parsley or cilantro, chopped
125 g sour cream
6 tortillas jumbo size
1/2 head lettuce finely shredded
1 carrot grated
200 g cheese mild or tasty, grated

Instructions

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker.
  2. Pound chicken breasts to 1cm thickness. Salt and pepper both sides.
  3. Mix all spices together. Coat chicken breasts with spice mix, set aside any leftover spices.
  4. Heat skillet on medium-high heat. Add oil when hot, wait until lightly smoking.
  5. Add chicken to pan. Cook 4-5 minutes, moving minimally, until browned, then flip and cook another 3-4 minutes until browned. Remove chicken and set aside, wrapped in foil.
  6. Saute onion until translucent. Add garlic and saute another 30 seconds.
  7. Add beans and corn to pan, along with any remaining spice mix, and reduce heat to medium. Cook until bean mix well heated through.
  8. Uncover chicken and chop roughly into smaller pieces. Toss back into pan, remove from heat, and stir to coat.
  9. Fluff rice and add parsley to rice, stirring in.
  10. Rinse and quickly clean and dry pan. Return pan to stove on medium heat.
  11. Heat a tortilla in pan quickly on both sides (no oil) until softened and very slightly puffy (don't brown).
  12. Place a couple of generous spoonfuls of rice on tortilla, then a couple of spoonfuls of chicken-bean mix, topped with other fillings to taste.
  13. Fold up bottom of tortilla, then both sides, then top. Place on counter, seam-side down.
  14. Repeat for all burritos.
  15. When all burritos stuffed, place 1tsp oil in hot pan, wait until heated, then place burritos two at a time, seam side down, in pan. Grill until lightly browned, then flip and grill other side.
  16. Serve immediately with hot sauce, salsa, guacamole, and/or additional sour cream.
