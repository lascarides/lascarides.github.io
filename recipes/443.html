Recipes » Main Course Easy as (chicken) pie

Ingredients

4 slices bread wholegrain or seeded
200 g chicken cooked, shredded
1 onion chopped
4 mushrooms sliced
4 eggs large
1 tsp oregano dried
3 potatoes chopped
1/4 cup trim milk
4 cups green beans cooked

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a medium-sized pie tin or round ceramic baking dish with baking paper. Arrange bread slices to cover bottom of dish. Scatter chicken over bread and top with onion and mushrooms. Crack eggs over vegetables.
  2. Step 2 Cover with tinfoil and bake for 45 minutes.
  3. Step 3 Meanwhile, steam potatoes for 8–10 minutes until tender. Drain well, add milk and mash until smooth.
  4. Step 4 Serve pie with mash and green beans.
