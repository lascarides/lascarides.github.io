Recipes » Salad Fruity rice salad

Ingredients

3/4 cups brown rice
1/2 cup pine nuts or pumpkin seeds
1/2 cup sultanas
1 stalk celery diced
4 spring onion chopped
1/2 cup walnuts pieces
1 Tbsp ginger finely chopped
2 apricot dried, chopped
dressing
1/4 cup orange juice
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 tsp ginger grated
2 tsp honey
1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Add the rice to a large pan of boiling water, cook uncovered for 30 minutes, or slightly less if you want a bit of crunch to your rice. Drain and cool.
  2. Step 2 Toast the pine nuts in a moderate oven for 5-8 minutes (keep an eye on them as they can burn quickly).
  3. Step 3 Combine all the dressing ingredients in a screw top jar and shake well to combine.
  4. Step 4 Combine all the ingredients in a salad bowl and toss well with the dressing to combine. Cover and refrigerate for several hours before serving; the flavour improves the longer you leave it.
