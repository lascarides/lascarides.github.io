Recipes » Main Course Recipe: Spinach and Ricotta Torte with Italian Cherry Tomatoes

Ingredients

1 cup ricotta cheese
1 bunch baby spinach
6 eggs
1 lemon zest of
1/3 cup rice flour brown
1 handful parmesan cheese grated
1 punnet cherry tomatoes
1 handful parsley italian flat leaf
olive oil
sea salt
pepper

Instructions

  1. *you could also use spelt flour, buckwheat flour, ground almonds or your choice of flour.
  2. 1. Preheat the oven to 170 C, and grease a cake tin with butter or olive oil.
  3. 2. Place the ricotta, spinach, eggs, lemon zest, flour, parmesan, and a large pinch of sea salt and pepper in to a food processor. Blend until smooth.
  4. 3. Pour the batter in to the prepared cake tin.
  5. 4. Slice each tomato in half, and then arrange on top of the torte.
  6. 5. Place in to the oven, and bake for 35-40 minutes or until cooked through.
  7. 6. Remove from the oven, and leave to cool before carefully inverting on to a plate.
  8. 7. Serve with a sprinkling of parsley, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.
