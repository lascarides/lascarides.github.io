Recipes » Main Course Lascarides Lamb Roast

Ingredients

lamb leg of
tomato paste
onions
garlic
potatoes

Instructions

  1. Salt and pepper the lamb, slather on a thick coating of tomato paste
  2. Throw some thin sliced onions & garlic in the bottom of the pan, add water around lamb (maybe a quarter of the way up)
  3. Stir in some more tomato paste.
  4. Cover and cook LOW AND SLOW - around 160C (150C fan) for four or more hours, depending on the size of the leg of lamb.
  5. Add potatoes around roast about an hour before it's finished.
  6. Keep an eye on the water, add more if necessary.
