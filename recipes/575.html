Recipes » Bread Tortillas

Ingredients

2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cups water
3 Tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Sift flour and salt into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add water and oil and mix to a smooth, pliable dough (can use Program 22 on breadmaker). Rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Cut the dough into 8–10 pieces, and roll each one out thin into a rough circle.
  4. Heat a frying pan to "pancake heat" and dry toast the tortilla on each side until slightly puffy and browned. Stack in warm oven and serve immediately.

Prep time 10 • Cook time 20
