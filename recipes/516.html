Recipes » Main Course Super Easy Peach and Cream Cheese Slice

Ingredients

100 g butter softened
1/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1.5 cups self-raising flour
1/2 cup desiccated coconut
1/4 cup milk
topping
820 g peaches canned, watties sliced in light syrup, well drained
250 g cream cheese
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg

Instructions

  1. Put the butter, sugar, eggs, flour, coconut and milk into a food processor and process until blended.
  2. Spread into a greased and lined 20 x 30cm slice tin.
  3. Arrange half the Wattie’s Peach Slices randomly on top.
  4. Blend the cream cheese, sugar and egg together in the food processor and pour over the top. Place remaining peach slices on top.
  5. Bake at 190°c for approximately 30 minutes until golden and well risen.
  6. Serve with natural or Greek yoghurt or ice-cream.

Prep time 15 • Cook time 30
