Recipes » Main Course Slow cooker sausages with lentils

Ingredients

4 sausages good quality spicy, more if you wish
1 onion large, chopped
2 cloves garlic large, chopped
2 stalks celery chopped
1 capsicum deseeded and chopped
800 g tomatoes chopped
1 Tbsp tomato paste
2 Tbsp pesto heaped
1.5 cups brown lentils uncooked
2 cups pumpkin diced
2 carrot peeled and chopped
1 tsp sugar
2 cups beef stock

Instructions

  1. Step 1 In a large frying pan brown the sausages. Toss in the onion, celery and capsicum and cook until beginning to soften. Add the tomatoes, tomato paste and 1 tablespoon of the pesto, then heat until simmering.
  2. Step 2 Remove the sausages from the pan and cut them into chunks before returning them to the mixture (if the sausages are fresh rather than dried, cut them at the end of the cooking).
  3. Step 3 Pour the mixture into the slow cooker, add the lentils, vegetables, stock and sugar and stir.
  4. Step 4 Cook on high for 6 hours or on low for 8 hours. When cooked, stir in the additional tablespoon of pesto and serve in warmed bowls with crusty bread.
