Recipes » Main Course Kashmiri Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp cardamom
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 chilli chopped
2 Tbsp ginger grated
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup potato diced into 2cm chunks
3/4 cups cauliflower
3/4 cups broccoli
3/4 cups pumpkin diced
1/2 cup carrot diced
2/3 cups yoghurt
2/3 cups vegetable stock
1 handful cashew toasted
1 handful coriander chopped
2 cups rice

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan and fry the ginger and chilli for 2 minutes, stirring continuously
  2. Add salt and spices and fry over a gentle heat for 2 minutes, stirring continuously
  3. Add the potatoes and stir to coat. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the rest of the veg and cook for a further 5 minutes.
  5. Add the yoghurt and veg stock, bring to the boil and then reduce heat to a simmer. Cover and cook for 10 minutes or until veg are cooked through.
  6. Serve with rice, sprinkle with cashew and coriander.
