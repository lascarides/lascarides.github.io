Recipes » Main Course Eggplant caponata with cheesy potato topping

Ingredients

4 potatoes large, peeled, sliced
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 onion chopped
1 clove garlic chopped
3 stalks celery chopped
1 eggplant diced
400 g tomatoes chopped
1 Tbsp tomato paste
1/2 cup black olives pitted, cut in half
1/4 cup capers
1/2 cup basil fresh, chopped
1 tsp butter
1 dash trim milk
1/2 cup parmesan cheese grated
salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Fill a large saucepan with cold water. Add potatoes and boil for 10 minutes until soft to mash. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Step 2 Meanwhile, in another large saucepan, heat oil on a medium heat and add onion and garlic. Sauté for 3-4 minutes. Add celery and eggplant and cook for 3 more minutes until vegetables soften and brown. Add tomatoes and tomato paste and simmer for 5 minutes until thickened. Stir in olives, capers and basil then remove from heat.
  3. Step 3 Drain then return potatoes to saucepan. Add spread and milk. Mash until smooth then add half the parmesan. Season to taste and combine well.
  4. Step 4 Pour tomato mixture into a large baking dish then pile the potato on top in even amounts and spread with a fork to cover. Sprinkle the remaining parmesan on top.
  5. Step 5 Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and serve.
