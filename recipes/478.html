Recipes » Main Course Bolognese Sauce

Ingredients

500 g beef mince lean
1 onion
6 cloves garlic
2 carrot
200 g tomato paste one tube
1 can tomatoes diced
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp oregano
1 tsp basil dried
1 tsp paprika
1 Tbsp worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook diced onions and carrots in oiled skillet on medium-high heat for about 6 minutes until onion is just browned and fragrant.
  2. 2. Add garlic and cook for an additional minute. Place vegetable mix in slow cooker.
  3. 3. Cook beef mince in skillet until browned.
  4. 4. Remove cooked mince from skillet with slotted spoon so fat remains in pan. Place cooked mince on double layer of paper towels to remove more fat.
  5. 5. Add mince to slow cooker.
  6. 6. Whisk tomato paste and 1/2 cup water together in bowl. Add paste, diced tomatoes, and all spices/herbs to slow cooker.
  7. 7. Give it a good stir and cook on low for 6-9 hours. Serve with pasta, cheese, fresh herbs and crusty bread.
