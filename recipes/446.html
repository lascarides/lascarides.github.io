Recipes » Desserts Courgette and chocolate shortcake

Ingredients

shortcake base
1.5 cups flour
1/2 cup brown sugar soft
1 tsp baking powder
125 g butter melted
topping
400 g condensed milk light sweetened
3 eggs
1 lemon zest of
2 Tbsp flour
1.5 cups courgette grated
1/4 cup chocolate chips dark
1/4 cup desiccated coconut

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 180ºC. Lightly grease a rectangular tin and line base with non-stick baking paper. Place shortcake base dry ingredients in a large bowl. Add melted spread and mix well. Press into base of tin. Bake for 10 minutes.
  2. Step 2 Meanwhile, place condensed milk and eggs in a large bowl and beat well. Add lemon zest, flour, courgettes and chocolate.
  3. Step 3 Pour over cooked base. Sprinkle over coconut. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until topping is set. Cool completely before cutting in squares.
