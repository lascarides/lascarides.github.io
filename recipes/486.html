Recipes » Main Course Misma's eggplant haloumi thing

Ingredients

1 eggplant
onion
fennel seeds
courgette
250 g halloumi
sunflower seeds
chia seeds
dates
orange juice or lemon juice
cavolo nero
salt and pepper
1.5 cups couscous palestinian trade aid
almonds

Instructions

  1. Eggplant and onion, fennel Seeds fried
  2. Courgette, fried
  3. Haloumi, in flour, fried
  4. Sunflower seeds, Chia seeds, toasted
  5. Dates soaked in Orange juice (or lemon juice)
  6. Cavolo Nero, oil and salt rubbed (raw)
  7. Salt and pepper
  8. Palestinian trade aid couscous
  9. almonds
