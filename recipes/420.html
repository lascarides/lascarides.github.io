Recipes » Main Course SPINACH, PESTO & SUN-DRIED TOMATO MUFFINS

Ingredients

250 g self-raising flour
1/4 tsp salt
25 g butter melted
1 egg large
150 ml yogurt vanilla or plain
180 ml milk
135 g pesto
25 g sun-dried tomatoes chopped or sliced
30 g baby spinach chopped or ripped to pieces (frozen or fresh)
115 g cheddar cheese grated
50 g parmesan cheese finely ground

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven at 180°C
  2. Combine flour and salt in a large mixing bowl, then make a well in the middle. Pour in the melted butter, egg, and milk.
  3. Fold lightly with a spatula. Make sure to break the yolk (or whisk the egg beforehand).
  4. Dump in the basil pesto. I prefer the chunky ones with cashews in them from the store because it’s ridiculously tasty, and with zero effort from me.
  5. At this point, add the sun-dried tomatoes and spinach (I used frozen free-flow ones – no need to chop. Bottom line is, I’m time-efficient. Okay, I’m lazy).
  6. Grate the cheddar cheese on top of everything. Throw in some Parmesan in there too.
  7. Spoon in some plain or vanilla yogurt.
  8. Mix lightly, we want soft muffins. We also want a sticky consistency, not too dry and not too wet.
  9. Scoop the mixture equally into muffin cases, then put them in a standard pan.
  10. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden brown and passes the toothpick test.
  11. Insert a toothpick in the middle and see if it comes away clean.
