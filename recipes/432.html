Recipes » Snacks and Sandwiches Green pea, feta and mint fritters

Ingredients

1/2 cup trim milk
2 eggs
1 cup self-raising flour
75 g feta cheese crumbled
2 cups peas frozen, thawed
2 courgette large, coarsely grated, squeezed of excess moisture
1/4 cup mint chopped
1 tsp lemon zest of
oil spray

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Whisk milk and eggs in a large bowl. Gradually whisk in flour until smooth. Stir through remaining ingredients.
  2. Step 2 Spray a large frying pan with oil and place over a medium-high heat. Spoon 1/4 cup mixture per fritter into pan, 3 fritters at a time. Cook for 3 minutes each side or until golden and cooked through.
  3. Step 3 Repeat to make 12 fritters, spraying pan with more oil if necessary.
