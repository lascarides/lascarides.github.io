Recipes » Main Course Libby's Spanish Chicken

Ingredients

chicken breast or thighs
1 onion
salt
pepper
smoked paprika
chorizo chopped
2 cans tomatoes
400 g chickpeas 1 can
garlic
carrot or eggplant
potatoes new, or packet rice

Instructions

  1. Chicken breast or thighs (I did thighs last night), roughly chopped (hastily chopped) onion, salt, pepper, generous heap of smoked paprika, chopped chorizo chunks, 2 tins of tomatoes and I added chickpeas too. Could add garlic. Baked 180 for about an hour.
  2. Could throw in carrot or eggplant
  3. Serve with new potatoes (or throw those in too) or packet rice. Would freeze.
