Recipes » Main Course Chicken and Green Bean Risotto

Ingredients

400 g chicken thighs
400 g green beans
1 leek
1 clove garlic
1 Tbsp butter
1 L chicken stock
3/4 cups arborio rice
4 Tbsp parmesan cheese grated

Instructions

  1. STEP 1 In a pan over medium heat, place 2 tablespoons of the oil and cook chicken until golden. Set aside.
  2. STEP 2 In a saucepan, add water or stock. Bring to the boil, then leave to simmer.
  3. STEP 3 In a large heavy based pan on medium heat, add remaining oil, butter, garlic and leeks, and fry until translucent.
  4. STEP 4 Add rice to the leek mixture and stir until rice is coated.
  5. STEP 5 Add a ladleful of stock and reduce heat to low, stirring continuously.
  6. STEP 6 As the liquid is absorbed, add another ladleful of liquid. Continue to do this for approximately 20 minutes or until rice is cooked al dente.
  7. STEP 7 Add salt and pepper.
  8. STEP 8 Add the chicken and green beans and heat through.
  9. STEP 9 When the mixture has a creamy texture, remove from heat, spoon into bowls and top with grated parmesan.
  10. Chicken breast can be used. Steam beans until just cooked. I used stringless beans. Water can replace chicken stock. Spring onion can be used.
