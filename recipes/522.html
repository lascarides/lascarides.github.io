Ingredients

1 bag tortilla chips natural (no seasoning)
1 onion chopped
2 cloves garlic
1 can chilli beans
1 can kidney beans or black beans
1 can corn kernels
200 g beef mince 80/20
150 g cheese mild or tasty, grated
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 jalapeno pepper optional
1 Tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 160 degrees C.
  2. Heat oil in skillet on medium high heat.
  3. Saute onions until translucent. Add garlic and saute for another 60 seconds, then add spices and stir well.
  4. Add beef mince and saute until no longer pink.
  5. Add all beans and corn, stir, and saute until hot. Remove from heat.
  6. In a separate cast iron skillet, place a layer of chips in the bottom, taking care to cover the surface but minimize overlaps.
  7. Layer a healthy scattering of cheese, then spoon a layer of the meat & bean filling, then another layer of cheese.
  8. Repeat process with a new layer of chips.
  9. Continue 3 or 4 times until pan is heaped with nachos and ingredients are used up.
  10. Top with a final layer of cheese and jalapeno (if desired).
  11. Place pan in oven and heat until cheese on top is melted, bubbly, and a bit browned.
  12. Serve immediately in sizzling pan on trivet. Provide serving spoon for digging out the gooey bits without burning fingers.
