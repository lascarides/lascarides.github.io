Recipes » Main Course Lentil Loaf

Ingredients

2 cups water
1 cup lentils
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 onion medium, finely diced
1 cup quick oats
1 tsp basil dried
1 tsp parsley dried
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
230 g tomato paste
1 egg slightly beaten
1/3 cup bbq sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the lentils.
  2. Bring the water to a boil, add the lentils, cover and cook over low heat for 40 minutes.
  3. Drain and place in a large bowl.
  4. Prepare the onions.
  5. Sauté the onion in the oil until tender. About half way from being fully cooked, toss in
  6. the basil, parsley, garlic powder, salt, and pepper and sauté with the onions.
  7. Mix together.
  8. Empty the pan of onions into the bowl with the lentils. Add the oats, tomato paste, and
  9. egg and mix by hand until well blended.
  10. Cook it.
  11. Place the mixture into a 9"x 5" loaf pan. If not non-stick, pre-treat the pan with oil.
  12. Flatten the loaf mixture into the pan and top the loaf with the BBQ sauce. Spread it
  13. around evenly.
  14. Bake the loaf at 180-degrees for 45 minutes. Let cook for 10 minutes before removing
  15. from the pan.
  16. Makes 6 servings.
