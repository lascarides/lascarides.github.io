Recipes » Main Course Chicken fajitas

Ingredients

8 tomatoes medium, ripe, peeled and chopped
1 onion large, chopped
1 clove garlic finely chopped
500 g chicken boneless, skinned
1 chilli pepper chopped
1 tsp chilli flakes
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp onion salt
1 tsp garlic chopped
1 tsp oregano
6 tortillas
100 g sour cream
1 cup lettuce shredded
1/2 cup cucumber diced
1 carrot shredded

Instructions

  1. Prepare tomatoes, onions and garlic and place in greased slow cooker
  2. Layer chicken pieces on top
  3. Sprinkle over chopped red pepper and seasonings
  4. Cover and cook on high for about 4 hours until chicken is falling apart and vegetables have cooked and thickened. If needed, can leave off lid towards end of cooking to reduce liquid.
  5. Shred meat and serve in flour tortillas with sides of cucumber, lettuce, avocado, lime wedges, yogurt or sour cream, chopped herbs, grated carrot or cheese, etc
