Recipes » Side Dishes Coleslaw

Ingredients

500 g cabbage finely chopped
1 carrot large, grated
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp brown sugar
2 Tbsp chia seeds

Instructions

  1. Combine cabbage and carrot in large mixing bowl
  2. Whisk mayo, vinegar, salt, sugar and chia seeds together in a small bowl
  3. Pour sauce mixture over veggies
  4. Mix well
  5. Chill in fridge 1 hour. Serve.
