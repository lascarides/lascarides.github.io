Recipes » Soup Slow Cooker Sausage and Potatoes

Ingredients

900 g sausage diced into 1-inch pieces
900 g potatoes washed and diced into chunks
1 onion large, sliced
8 cloves garlic
1 can cream of chicken soup
3/4 cups chicken stock
2 Tbsp worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. Lightly oil or butter your crock pot insert
  2. Layer the potatoes, onions, then the sausage
  3. Mix the soup, broth and Worcestershire sauce in a separate bowl and pour over the top
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours
  5. , Freezer Meal Directions:
  6. Toss the sausage, onions and garlic into a zippable freezer bag.
  7. Mix the broth, Worcestershire sauce and soup. Pour over sausage mixture.
  8. Lay flat, squeeze out air and label.
  9. Place a note on your label (and your calendar) that you'll need potatoes for this recipe.
  10. , To Cook Freezer Meal:
  11. Defrost overnight in the refrigerator.
  12. Butter crock pot insert.
  13. Pour washed, diced potatoes in bottom.
  14. Pour freezer bag contents over the top.
  15. Cook until potatoes are tender. About 7 hours.
