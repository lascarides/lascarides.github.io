Recipes » Baking Spinach Cheddar Muffins

Ingredients

1.5 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp green herb seasoning
1/2 tsp salt
6 Tbsp butter melted
1 egg
1 cup milk
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
1/2 cup spinach frozen, drained and squeezed dry
1 cup cheddar cheese shredded

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease 12 cup muffin cups.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda, spices, and salt together in a mixing bowl.
  3. Stir the melted butter, oil, egg, milk, spinach, and Cheddar cheese together in a large mixing bowl until evenly blended. Slowly stir in the flour mixture to form a batter. Spoon about 2 tablespoons into each muffin cup.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 35 minutes.
