Recipes » Desserts Poached Quinces

Ingredients

2 Kg quince
1 Kg sugar
1.5 L water
1/4 cup lemon juice
100 g honey
1 quill cinnamon
1 star anise
1 pod vanilla
1 lemon peel of

Instructions

  1. Make the syrup by dissolving the sugar and water and then bring to the boil.
  2. Heat the oven to 160C
  3. Remove from the heat and add the honey stirring to dissolve
  4. Add the remaining ingredients
  5. In a roasting dish place the quinces cut in half ( not need to peel or core)
  6. Pour the warm syrup and spices over the quinces
  7. Put a sheet of silicon paper on top of the quinces, and the liquid, then cover the roasting dish with foil
  8. Place on the middle shelf in the oven and bake for 2 hours.
  9. After an hour remove the baking dish and check turn the quinces with a large spoon
  10. Remove from the oven and allow to cool
  11. Quinces
  12. Have a rich fragrance and a sticky skin, there are many different varieties but three shapes of fruit known as “quince” The squat apple shaped, the pear shaped and the larger “pawpaw “ shaped quince. They are increasingly available in fruit shops and markets.
