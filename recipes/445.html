Recipes » Salad Pear and walnut bread

Ingredients

3/4 cups self-raising flour
3/4 cups wholemeal flour
3/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup caster sugar
2 bananas medium, mashed
1 egg lightly beaten
1/4 cup olive oil light
1/4 cup trim milk
1 pear peeled, cored and chopped
2 stalks rhubarb thinly sliced
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup walnuts coarsely chopped

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 190°C. Line a 21cm x 8cm loaf tin with baking paper.
  2. Step 2 In a large bowl combine flours, baking powder and castor sugar. In a separate bowl combine bananas, egg, oil and milk. Fold into flour mixture then gently fold in pear and rhubarb.
  3. Step 3 Spoon into prepared tin and sprinkle with brown sugar, cinnamon and walnuts. Bake for 1 hour or until an inserted skewer comes out clean. Stand for 5 minutes in tin before turning out on a wire rack to cool.
