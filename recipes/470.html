Recipes » Breakfast Zucchini Feta Muffins

Ingredients

2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
pinches salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup olive oil
1.5 cups courgette shredded
1 cup feta cheese crumbled
1.5 Tbsp lemon zest of
pine nuts
basil leaves
sun-dried tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 12 cup muffin pan.
  2. Sift the dry ingredients together in a medium bowl. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and oil with an electric mixer until smooth.
  3. Stir in the zucchini, feta cheese, and lemon zest. Add the dry ingredients and stir until the mixture is just combined.
  4. Scoop the mixture into the prepared pan using an ice cream scoop for portion control. Bake for 15-30 minutes depending on your desired level of darkness. Remove the pan from the oven and cool for 5 minutes. Transfer the muffins to a rack and cool for another 20 minutes.
  5. Store refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 2 days or freeze for up to 3 months.
  6. Number of Servings: 16
