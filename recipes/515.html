Recipes » Baking Banana Peach Bread

Ingredients

2 cups flour
1.5 cups white sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp cinnamon
0.125 tsp allspice
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs
1/3 cup oil
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup bananas mashed
1 cup peaches chopped into small pieces
3 Tbsp cinnamon sugar

Instructions

  1. Half the sugar, makes 2 loaves
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  3. In a medium bowl combine flour, sugar, soda, powder and spices. Make well in center.
  4. In small bowl, whisk eggs. Whisk in oil, vanilla and banana.
  5. Pour wet ingredients over dry and stir just until moistened.
  6. Fold in peaches. Do not over mix.
  7. Divide between two greased 8x4" loaf pans. Sprinkle each with 1 1/2 T cinnamon sugar.
  8. Bake 40-50 minutes or until tootpick comes out clean.
