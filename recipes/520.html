Ingredients

450 g flour
1 Tbsp sugar
4 tsp yeast edmonds surebake
230 g water
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tsp salt
1 can tomato paste small
400 g mozzarella cheese shredded
100 g salami
50 g parmesan cheese grated
1 tsp oregano
1 can pineapple pieces
70 g ham

Instructions

  1. 1. Mix water, sugar, and yeast in the breadmaker. Let stand for 10 minutes
  2. 2. Add remaining ingredients
  3. 3. Run program 22 on breadmaker
  4. 4. When completed, drizzle olive oil on dough and place in a stainless bowl.
  5. 5. Cover bowl snugly with plastic wrap and place in refrigerator to rise. The longer the better, 12-48 hours.
  6. 6. Uncooked dough can be wrapped and frozen at any point in the process.
  7. 7. Make pizza! Alternatively, stretch dough on to a sheet and sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmesan and 1 tablespoon minced rosemary to make breadsticks (excellent with soup).
