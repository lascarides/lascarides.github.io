Recipes » Snacks and Sandwiches CHEDDAR & ROCKET SCONES

Ingredients

450 g flour
4 baking powder
3/4 tsp salt
3/4 tsp pepper
1 small pinch cayenne pepper
115 g butter cold, diced
300 ml milk
2.5 cups cheese tasty, grated
40 g rocket roughly chopped
0.125 cups milk for glazing

Instructions

  1. CHEDDAR & ROCKET SCONES
  2. Cheesey and delicious these scones can be made with other strong cheese and whatever herbs you like. The trick is to be gentle and have the mix not to dry or wet – no pressure now!
  3. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and pepper
  4. Rub the butter into flour, either by hand or in a standing mixer with the K beater attachment fitted .
  5. Add the grated tasty cheese & chopped rocket.
  6. Mix a little until the cheese and rocket are lightly coated in flour.
  7. Add milk mix and allow the dough to just come together.
  8. Remove from the bowl or mixer and turn out onto a lightly floured bench. Gently and lightly bring the lumpy mix together.
  9. Roll out into an oblong pushing the dough at the sides to get the oblong the same thickness all over.
  10. Roll out into a large rectangle just larger than a Swiss roll tin and cut 3 by 8
  11. Brush with milk for glazing and sprinkle lightly with cheese
  12. Bake at 190 until cooked 15 -20 minutes
  13. Cut into 3 by 8 Rectangles
  14. POINTS TO NOTE
  15. Use cold butter and milk and mix lightly
  16. The mix needs to be wet but not sloppy
  17. The oven hot
