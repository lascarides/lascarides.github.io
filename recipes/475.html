Recipes » Main Course Beetroot Risotto

Ingredients

4 Tbsp butter or olive oil
1 onion brown, roughly chopped
4 cloves garlic roughly chopped
1 cup brown rice medium grain
500 g beetroot peeled and grated
3 cups vegetable stock
3/4 cups parmesan cheese finely grated
thyme to garnish

Instructions

  1. Melt 2 Tbsp butter in large saucepan over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, cook,
  2. stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes, or until the onion is soft and slightly browned.
  3. Add the rice and cook stirring gently, for 5 minutes, or until the grains are toasted and
  4. fragrant.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, then add beetroot, stock and 2 cups water. Simmer, uncovered,
  6. for about 45 mins, until almost all of the liquid has been absorbed.
  7. Stir in the remaining butter and half the parmesan. Add a large pinch of sea salt and
  8. freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  9. Divide the risotto among four bowls. Serve warm, sprinkled with thyme and remaining
  10. parmesan.
