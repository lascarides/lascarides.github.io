Recipes » Main Course Slow-cooked lamb ragoût with tagliatelle

Ingredients

1 Tbsp olive oil
1 onion medium, finely chopped
2 stalks celery finely chopped
1 carrot large, chopped
1 parsnip large, chopped
3 cloves garlic crushed
450 g lamb lean (eg. lamb shoulder), diced
2 Tbsp tomato paste
400 g tomatoes chopped
1 cup beef stock
2 cups water
500 g tagliatelle fresh
1/4 cup parmesan cheese finely shaved
parsley to garnish

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Heat oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat. Add onion, celery, carrot, parsnip and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes. Increase heat to high. Add lamb and cook for 5 minutes or until browned all over. Add tomato paste. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  2. Step 2 Add tomatoes, stock and water to saucepan. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Simmer, partially covered, for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Step 3 When ragoût is almost cooked, cook pasta following packet directions. Mix half the ragoût through the pasta then spoon into bowls. Top bowls with remaining ragoût. Sprinkle with parmesan and serve garnished with parsley.
