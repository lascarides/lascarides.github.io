Recipes » Main Course Mushroom and spinach risotto

Ingredients

1 Tbsp olive oil
4 cups mushrooms sliced (i used swiss browns)
1 rice herby onion
50 g cheese semi-soft , cubed (i used fruity port salut - brie or camembert work well, too)
baby spinach big handful
rice herby onion
2 onions thinly sliced
1 Tbsp thyme chopped fresh, or rosemary
salt and pepper
1.5 cups rice medium-grain
3 cups vegetable stock

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Heat the oil in a large heavy-based saucepan. Add the mushrooms, season with salt and pepper and fry for 5 minutes until golden and juicy. Add the herby onion rice and cook, stirring, for 5 minutes or until hot through. Add a splash of hot water if it starts to stick.
  2. Step 2 Add the cheese and spinach and heat for 1 minute, stirring, then divide among 4 bowls and serve.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large heavy-based saucepan. Add onions and thyme or rosemary. Season and cook gently for 15 minutes, stirring regularly, until the onions are soft. (Cooking the onions slowly brings out their gorgeous sweetness.) Add rice and vegetable stock then cover and bring to the boil. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is almost cooked. (Under-cook the rice slightly so it retains some bite, as it will finish cooking in the recipe.)
  4. You can also use this rice as a base in these recipes:
