Recipes » Desserts Bex's Birthday Cake

Ingredients

3 cups sugar
2 cups water
1 cup cocoa powder
250 g butter
4 eggs
3 cups self-raising flour
500 g chocolate whittaker's 50% dark block
1 cup cream

Instructions

  1. Okay!
  2. Grab a big ass pot
  3. Put
  4. 3c sugar
  5. 2c water
  6. 1c cocoa powder
  7. 250g butter
  8. In the pot
  9. Bring to a simmer and get all the sugar to dissolve.
  10. Take off the heat and let it cool down for a while.
  11. Then add 1tsp baking soda and stir.
  12. Then add
  13. 4 eggs
  14. 3 cups of self raising flour, or just normal flour with 3t of baking powder. Probably easiest to mix with a hand mixer thing but you can just use a spoon.
  15. Pour into a lined roasting pan, bake at 180'ish for about 25-30 mins. If you jiggle the pan it won't wobble heaps and that's how you can see it's done. It's self levelling and tastes better a few days after being baked.
  16. ganache
  17. Easiest is to grab a whittakers 50%dark block, break it up and then 1c of simmered cream
  18. You can just put it in a container in the fridge til you need it
