Recipes » Breakfast Lascarides Super Muesli

Ingredients

400 g oats
100 g almonds chopped
70 g coconut shredded
70 g sunflower seeds
50 g pumpkin seeds
50 g chia seeds
3 apple cored and grated
1 cup apple juice or orange juice
100 g dates pitted and chopped
100 g raisins
1 can coconut cream
1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Place all oats, almonds, shredded coconut, and seeds in a roasting pan and toast in oven at 150 degrees C until toasted and fragrant. Stir occasionally, don't over-toast.
  2. Grate apple into large mixing bowl, then add juice, dates, raisins, vanilla, and coconut cream. Stir until just combined.
  3. When dry items are toasted, remove from oven and stir into mixing bowl until just combined.
  4. Add 1 cup or so boiling water if needed, until mixture is just porridgey.
  5. When cooled, transfer to container and place in fridge overnight.
  6. To serve: scoop into bowl, add milk to taste, and microwave on high for 60 seconds.
  7. Top with fresh fruit, yogurt, cream, brown sugar, and/or a pat of butter, as desired.
