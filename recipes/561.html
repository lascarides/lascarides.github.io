Recipes » Salad Green Beans Tossed with Walnut-Miso Sauce

Ingredients

4 cups green beans trimmed (about 10 ounces)
2 Tbsp ginger grated peeled fresh
3 Tbsp walnuts toasted, coarsely chopped
2 Tbsp water
1.5 Tbsp miso yellow
1/2 tsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Place beans into a large saucepan of boiling water; cook 5 minutes. Drain and plunge beans into ice water; drain.
  2. Place ginger on several layers of damp cheesecloth. Gather edges of cheesecloth; squeeze cheesecloth bag over a small bowl. Set 1/2 teaspoon ginger juice aside; reserve remaining juice for another use. Discard ginger.
  3. Combine 1/2 teaspoon ginger juice, walnuts, water, miso, and soy sauce in a food processor; process until nuts are minced. Combine walnut mixture and beans in a large bowl.
