Recipes » Main Course Chunky beef curry

Ingredients

1 Tbsp oil
1 onion large, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic finely crushed
1 Tbsp ginger grated
2 Tbsp curry powder mild
600 g beef lean, cubed
1 tsp salt
420 g tomatoes chopped
1 cube beef stock mixed with 1 cup boiling water
1 cup coconut milk light
500 g potatoes halved
2 Tbsp parsley chopped fresh

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Heat oil and add onion. Cook over a medium heat until softened. Add garlic and ginger. Cook for a few minutes. Stir in curry powder and heat for 1 minute.
  2. Step 2 Add beef. Cook, stirring over a high heat, until meat is well coated with spice mixture and browned all over.
  3. Step 3 Add salt, tomatoes and stock. Cover and simmer for 1 to 1 1/4 hours, or until meat is tender.
  4. Step 4 Add coconut milk and potatoes. Heat for 5 more minutes. Garnish with parsley. Serve with mini naan or pita bread.
