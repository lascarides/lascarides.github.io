Recipes » Main Course Japanese Hot Pot (Sukiyaki)

Ingredients

112.5 g sweet potato noodles
8 cups water warm
1 Tbsp coconut oil or tallow
225 g beef sirloin, thinly sliced
90 g mushrooms shiitake, sliced and stems removed
2.5 cups beef stock
1/4 cup white wine dry
6 Tbsp coconut aminos
1/4 cup coconut sugar
112.5 g baby bok choy cut in half
1 tomatoes beefsteak, quartered

Instructions

  1. Soak the sweet potato noodles in warm water for at least half an hour to soften the noodles and make them easier to cook.
  2. Melt coconut oil (or tallow) in a large wok over medium-heat. Add the sirloin and sauté for 1 minute until it starts to brown. Remove and set aside. Add the shiitake mushrooms and sauté for 2 minutes. Add the beef bone broth, white wine, coconut aminos and coconut sugar. Stir and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium. Add the sweet potato noodles and return the cooked beef to the wok. Simmer for 2 minutes. Add the baby bok choy and tomato quarters. Simmer for 5 more minutes until the bok choy is cooked yet crips and the tomato quarters are soft. Remove form the heat and serve immediately.
  4. Note: removed extra sugar, subbed some water instead of broth, fresh mushrooms instead of shiitake, added firm silken tofu, used chinese glass noodles
