Ingredients

2 eggs
3/4 cups macadamia nut oil cold pressed, or lightly flavoured olive oil
1/2 cup agave syrup
1 cup yogurt plain greek
1 tsp vanilla
3 lemon zest of
2 cups wholemeal flour
2 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celsius (350F). Line a loaf tin with baking paper. Place the eggs, oil, agave, yoghurt, vanilla and lemon zest into a large bowl and whisk to combine. Add the flour and baking powder and stir to combine. Pour the batter into the lined loaf tin and bake for 45-50 minutes or until the loaf bounces back to the touch. Allow to cool for 5 minutes or so in the tin before removing to cool completely on a wire rack. Enjoy x
