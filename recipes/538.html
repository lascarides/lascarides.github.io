Recipes » Main Course Sushi rolls

Ingredients

6 sheets nori
3 cups sushi rice
1 granny smith apple julienned
1 carrot large, julienned
200 g smoked salmon or tuna
150 g cucumber cut into strips
1/4 cup sushi vinegar
400 g shiitake mushrooms sliced
1 Tbsp canola oil
1 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp soy sauce divided
1 Tbsp mirin
500 g edamame frozen (one package)
salt

Instructions

  1. Wash rice thoroughly until water runs clear, then cook in rice cooker.
  2. Prepare all vegetables and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat. When shimmering and hot, add mushroom to pan. Cook until moisture has sweated out.
  4. When mushrooms have cooked down, add mirin and 1 tsp of the soy sauce. Stir in for 30 seconds or so, then remove pan from heat and stir in butter.
  5. Remove rice from heat and stir in sushi vinegar. Wait until rice cools to room temperature.
  6. Using a sushi roll mat, cover one piece of nori with a thin layer of rice, spreading evenly. Add fillings (to taste) in a strip and roll up into a roll. Press together firmly, then set aside. Repeat until out of fillings. Cut each roll into 6 pieces.
  7. Boil edamame in water for 2 minutes, then drain well and toss in a bowl with salt to taste.
  8. Serve immediately with soy sauce for dipping.
