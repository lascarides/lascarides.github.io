Recipes » Main Course Moroccan Beef Meatballs

Ingredients

1/4 cup cauliflower grated
250 g beef mince
1 egg small
1 Tbsp parsley flat-leaf, chopped
1/2 tsp coriander
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp ginger
1 onion small, finely chopped
1 Tbsp garlic minced
1 can tomato pulsed
1/2 cup beef stock
1 Tbsp honey
salt and pepper
1/2 cup couscous

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add the cauliflower, mince, coriander, spices, half of the onion, and half of the garlic. Mix roughly by hand.
  2. Form mixture into golfball-sized balls and place them in either a heavy-based pan or slow cooker.
  3. Heat some oil in a saucepan and add the remaining onion and garlic. Sautee until onion is translucent (3–5 minutes) taking care not to burn the garlic.
  4. Add the S&P, tomato & paste, honey, and stock. Stir to combine and heat through.
  5. Pour the sauce over the meatballs. Bring to the boil and then simmer covered for a long time (or just slow-cook on low for 4–6 hours).
  6. Before serving, steep the couscous in 1 cup boiling water and leave for 15 minutes (or until all of the liquid is absorbed).
  7. Serve meatballs over the couscous with a dribble of yoghurt and a sprinkle of parsley.
