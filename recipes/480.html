Recipes » Baking Cup of Brownie

Ingredients

1.5 Tbsp butter melted
2 Tbsp flour
2 Tbsp caster sugar
1 Tbsp cocoa powder
1.5 Tbsp water
1 drop vanilla
sprinkles salt

Instructions

  1. Measure butter into mug and melt in microwave
  2. Measure remaining ingredients on top of melted butter. Mix well.
  3. microwave on high for 1 min, or until set.
