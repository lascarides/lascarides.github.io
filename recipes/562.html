Recipes » Baking Taieri Apple Pudding

Ingredients

3 apples peeled & chopped
1 cup sugar
150 g butter melted
1 egg
1.5 cups flour
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp mixed spice
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup walnuts
1 cup sultanas

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 deg C
  2. Put apples into a bowl
  3. Sprinkle over sugar
  4. Pour over butter
  5. Break egg into the mixture & stir well
  6. Add remaining ingredients & mix well
  7. Tip into baking dish
  8. Bake for 40mins or until a skewer comes out clean.
  9. Enjoy! PS It's fairly sweet so could cut back on the sugar a bit.
