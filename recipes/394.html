Recipes » Beverages Lemon barley water

Ingredients

3/4 cups pearl barley
2 lemon zest and juice
1/2 cup honey

Instructions

  1. Rinse ¾ cup of pearl barley under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Place the barley in a saucepan with the zest of 2 lemons and 6 cups of water.
  3. Bring to the boil and then simmer for 10 minutes, strain and discard the barley.
  4. Stir in the juice of the 2 lemons and half a cup of honey. Cool to room temperature. This is ready to serve, and you can keep the remainder in the fridge.
