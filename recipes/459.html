Recipes » Main Course Garlic-ginger vege stir-fry

Ingredients

2 onions diced or chopped
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tsp ginger grated
1 chilli pepper medium red, seeded, chopped
8 cups mixed vegetables such as bok choy, broccolini, snow peas, mushrooms and bean sprouts
1 lime juice of
1/2 cup coriander chopped fresh, to serve

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Heat a large, oiled frying pan or wok to a high heat. Add onion. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  2. Step 2 Add garlic, ginger and chilli. Cook for 2 more minutes until softened.
  3. Step 3 Add the remaining vegetables and stir-fry until tender crisp. Serve with lime juice and coriander.
