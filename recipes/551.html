Recipes » Bread Michael's basic breadmaker bread

Ingredients

275 g flour
175 g wholemeal flour
4 tsp yeast
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp butter
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tsp sugar
1 Tbsp chia seeds
1 Tbsp sunflower seeds
270 ml water

Instructions

  1. Mix water, yeast and sugar in breadmaker pan. Let sit for 10 minutes to activate yeast.
  2. Add all other ingredients.
  3. For bread, run program 1.
  4. For dough, run program 16. Proof bread in fridge overnight. Brush with milk or beaten egg, then bake in preheated dutch oven at 180-200 degrees for 10-20 minutes.
