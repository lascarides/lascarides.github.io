Recipes » Snacks and Sandwiches Courgette cupcakes with pineapple frosting

Ingredients

1.5 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1.5 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup caster sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
150 g butter
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup sour cream or low-fat yoghurt
1.25 cups courgette grated
frosting
250 g cream cheese
2 Tbsp maple syrup
1/2 cup pineapple crushed , drained

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 180ºC. Line muffin tin holes with paper cases or lightly grease mini-loaf tins.
  2. Step 2 Sift flour, salt, cinnamon and baking soda into a bowl and mix together lightly. Set aside.
  3. Step 3 Beat sugar and spread together. Add eggs one at a time. Stir in vanilla, sour cream and courgettes.
  4. Step 4 Fold in flour. Spoon into cases or mini-loaf tins and bake for 25-30 minutes or until firm and golden.
  5. Step 5 Cool completely. To make frosting, beat cream cheese with syrup. Use to ice cakes then top with a dollop of pineapple.
