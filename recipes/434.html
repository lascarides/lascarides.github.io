Recipes » Main Course Chicken and pea pasta bake

Ingredients

250 g macaroni pasta
oil spray
1 onion medium, finely chopped
1 leek small, trimmed, sliced thinly
1 cup mixed vegetables frozen pea and corn mix
1.5 cups chicken shredded, skinless, cooked
2 Tbsp flour
375 ml evaporated milk light and creamy
1/2 cup cheddar cheese
oregano fresh, chopped

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 200°C. Cook pasta following packet directions. Drain. Set aside.
  2. Step 2 Meanwhile, spray a large non-stick frying pan with oil. Heat over a medium heat. Add onion and leek. Cook for 5 minutes, or until softened. Add vegetables and chicken. Cook for 3-4 minutes. Add flour. Stir to combine, then add milk and simmer for 1-2 minutes, or until warmed through. Add pasta. Toss to coat. Remove from heat.
  3. Step 3 Transfer pasta mixture to 4, 2-cup capacity ovenproof dishes. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden. To serve, sprinkle with oregano (if using).
