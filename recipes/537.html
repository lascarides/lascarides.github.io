Recipes » Side Dishes Aunt Dorothy's Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

625 g cheese approx (cheddar, tasty, mild, etc... sharper the better)
500 g rotini
1 can evaporated milk small
100 g butter approx
salt and pepper
1/2 tsp paprika

Instructions

  1. Cut 3/4 of the cheese into 2cm cubes.
  2. Cook pasta in liberally salted water until tender. Quickly drain and return to warm pot while still moist. Add the milk and cubed cheese and stir over low heat until cheese is melted.
  3. Add butter, salt and pepper to taste. Stir until incorporated.
  4. Divide into two 2L glass casserole dishes. Slice the remaining cheese thinly and cover the top of the pasta. Sprinkle with paprika.
  5. Bake at 190C for 30 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
