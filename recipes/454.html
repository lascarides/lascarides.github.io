Recipes » Side Dishes Lentil and corn fritters with tomato and avocado salsa

Ingredients

salsa tomato and avocado
1 avocado large (or 2 small), diced
2 tomatoes large (or 4 small), diced
2 spring onion finely sliced
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp sweet chilli sauce
fritters
400 g brown lentils drained and rinsed
400 g creamed corn
1 cup self-raising flour
1 spring onion large (or 2 small), finely sliced
oil spray to grease

Instructions

  1. Step 1 To make salsa, mix avocados, tomatoes and spring onions together in a bowl. Mix lemon juice and sweet chilli sauce together then stir through.
  2. Step 2 Place fritter ingredients in a bowl and mix to combine. Heat a heavy-based frying pan over a medium heat with a little oil and fry spoonfuls of batter until bubbles appear on the top surface. Flip to cook the underside. Repeat the process until all the batter is cooked. Serve with salsa.
