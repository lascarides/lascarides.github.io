Ingredients

2 avocado
2 tomatoes chopped and seeded
1/2 onion white or red, finely chopped
1 lemon juice of, or lime
1 tsp sea salt
to_tastes cayenne pepper
to_tastes black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mash avocado. Mix in tomato, then onion, then lemon/lime, then spices.
  2. Stir until just combined.
  3. Serve with tortilla chips, or as a side for taco/burrito night.
