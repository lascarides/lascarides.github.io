Recipes » Main Course Empire Meatloaf

Ingredients

750 g beef mince lean
1 red onion peeled and finely chopped
2 tsp garlic crushed
1 Tbsp thyme
2 Tbsp parsley chopped
1.25 cups breadcrumbs fresh, white
2 eggs beaten
4 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
400 g tomatoes wattie's indian style
1/2 cup beef stock or wine or water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (fan bake).
  2. In a large bowl, combine beef mince, onion, garlic, thyme, parsley, breadcrumbs, eggs, 3 tablespoons Lea and Perrins , and half a can Wattie’s Indian Style Tomatoes , season well with salt and pepper.
  3. Using your hands or a wooden spoon, mix well. Shape the mixture into a loaf shape and place in a 7-8 cup capacity lasagne-style dish.
  4. Combine the remaining 1⁄2 can Wattie’s Indian Style Tomatoes with 1 tablespoon Lea and Perrins and beef stock, mix well and pour over the loaf. Bake for 45 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through. Rest the meatloaf for 5 minutes before carving thick slices.
  5. Serve with mashed potatoes and steamed green vegetables.

Prep time 10 • Cook time 45
