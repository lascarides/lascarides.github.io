Recipes » Baking Easy biscuits

Ingredients

250 g butter whole block
3 cups wholemeal flour
2 cups flour
1/2 cup custard flour
5 tsp baking powder
395 g condensed milk sweetened, whole can

Instructions

  1. Make up batch, divide into 4 portions, add whatever flavours you like...
  2. lemon, poppyseed
  3. sultana, oat
  4. apricot, almond
  5. date, coconut etc
  6. Cream butter and condensed milk in mixer, add dry ingredients.
  7. 9 minutes at 180
