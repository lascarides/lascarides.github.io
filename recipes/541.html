Recipes » Snacks Homemade crackers

Ingredients

1.25 cups flour whole wheat, fresh ground, or all-purpose
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp smoked paprika
4 Tbsp butter
1/4 cup water
1 Tbsp honey
1 pinch sea salt to sprinkle on top

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C
  2. Measure out dry ingredients into large mixing bowl. Cut in butter until it looks like pea sized clumps. Add in water and honey, stir until just combined.
  3. Lightly flour a baking sheet or stone. Roll dough out on it. Use a pizza cutter and cut into desired shapes. Sprinkle with sea salt.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes!
  5. If you don’t roll the cracker dough thin enough, they won’t be crunchy, but still delectable.
  6. The edges will be thinner, remove them and bake remaining crackers for another 2 minutes.
  7. Leave crackers on baking sheet to cool, they’ll crisp up as they cool
  8. To make these a soaked traditional recipe, add 1 Tablespoon apple cider vinegar to the recipe and let sit for 8 to 12 hours before rolling out.
