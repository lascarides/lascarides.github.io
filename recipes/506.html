Recipes » Main Course Quick paneer and chickpea curry

Ingredients

1 Tbsp vegetable oil plus 1 tsp
225 g paneer cut into 2cm cubes
1 onion large, finely sliced
2 cloves garlic crushed
1 Tbsp ginger grated
3 Tbsp curry paste patak’s mild
1 can tomatoes chopped
1 can chickpeas drained
300 g spinach frozen
1 Tbsp lemon juice
2.5 cups basmati rice and natural yogurt
1 can baked beans

Instructions

  1. Heat the 1 tbsp oil in a large lidded frying pan over a high heat. Add the paneer and fry for about 5 minutes until golden on all sides.
  2. Remove the paneer from the pan using a slotted spoon and put on a plate lined with kitchen paper.
  3. Add the extra teaspoon of oil to the same pan, then fry the onion, garlic and ginger until the onion has softened.
  4. Add the curry paste and cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, chickpeas, beans, and spinach, then cook over a low-medium heat for 10 minutes with the lid on, stirring occasionally, until the spinach has thawed and the curry is warmed through.
  6. Turn the heat down, then stir in the paneer, lemon juice and a generous pinch of salt and pepper.
  7. Serve with steamed basmati rice and a dollop of yogurt on top.
