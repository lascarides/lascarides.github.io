Recipes » Salad Moroccan carrot salad

Ingredients

4 carrot medium, peeled and grated
1/2 red onion thinly sliced
2 Tbsp currants
1/4 cup parsley chopped flat-leaf
dressing
1/3 cup lemon juice
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 tsp honey
1 tsp cumin ground
1 tsp ground coriander
black pepper

Instructions

  1. Step 1 In a bowl gently toss together salad ingredients.
  2. Step 2 In a separate bowl whisk together dressing ingredients. Just before serving, toss dressing through.
