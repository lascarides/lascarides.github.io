Recipes » Breakfast Cinnamon and banana pikelets

Ingredients

1 cup self-raising flour
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2/3 cups trim milk
1 egg lightly beaten
oil spray
1 bananas large, cut in 24 slices

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Sift dry ingredients into a large bowl. Whisk in milk and egg until smooth.
  2. Step 2 Spray a frying pan with oil. To make pikelets, drop 2 tablespoons batter into pan. Top with 3 banana slices. Cook over a medium heat for 1-2 minutes until bubbles appear on surface. Flip and cook for 1-2 more minutes. Repeat process to make 8 pikelets. Serve with reduced-fat ice cream if preferred.
