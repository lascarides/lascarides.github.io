Recipes » Main Course Chicken Korma

Ingredients

1 onion
2 Tbsp tomato paste
2 cups jasmine rice
2 Tbsp olive oil
150 g korma spice paste (1/2 jar)
400 ml coconut milk
50 ml water
1/2 tsp garlic chopped
1/2 tsp ginger chopped
500 g chicken breast diced
50 ml cream

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a med saucepan. Add onion and cook until soft.
  2. Add Patak’s Korma Spice Paste, tomato paste, ginger and garlic. Stir occasionally, until reduced and thickened.
  3. Add 50ml water, bring to the boil, cover, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add chicken and fry until sealed.
  5. Add coconut milk, double cream and a pinch of sugar (optional)
  6. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh chopped coriander or toasted coconut flakes/chips.
