Recipes » Main Course Spaghetti with courgettes, feta, mint and pine nuts

Ingredients

400 g spaghetti
5 courgette small, trimmed, thinly shredded lengthways
150 g feta cheese crumbled
1 bunch mint
2 Tbsp pine nuts toasted

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Cook pasta in a large saucepan following packet directions until al dente. Drain and return to saucepan.
  2. Step 2 Meanwhile, bring a large pan of water to the boil. Add courgettes. Cook for 1-2 minutes, or until just tender. Drain. Add courgettes, feta, mint and pine nuts to pasta. Toss until well combined. Serve immediately.
