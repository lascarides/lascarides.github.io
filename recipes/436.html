Recipes » Main Course Vegetarian sausage stir-fry

Ingredients

5 sausages vegetarian such as tomato and olive-flavoured, sliced
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp ginger puréed
400 g mixed vegetables stir-fry
400 g brown rice
3 Tbsp teriyaki sauce or hoisin sauce
6 Tbsp orange juice

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Slice sausages. Heat a frying pan with a little oil and add sausages. Cook for a few minutes with ginger purée until browned.
  2. Step 2 Add vegetables with a little more oil. Stir-fry for a few more minutes.
  3. Step 3 Add rice and cook for 2 minutes. Mix teriyaki sauce with orange juice. Add to pan and heat through. Serve.
