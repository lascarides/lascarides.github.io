Recipes » Baking Carrot, spinach and pumpkin seed muffins

Ingredients

1 Tbsp oil
1 onion finely chopped
2 tsp cumin ground
100 g baby spinach stalks removed
250 g self-raising flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
2 eggs
1 cup yogurt plain
1 cup carrot grated
1/3 cup corn kernels
1/3 cup pumpkin seeds

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Line a 12-hole muffin tin with paper cases or lightly grease tin with oil spray. Preheat oven to 200°C. Heat oil in a large non-stick pan and cook onion until softened. Add cumin and cook for a few more minutes.
  2. Step 2 Add spinach with 4 tablespoons of hot water and cook until wilted. Cool onion and spinach for a few minutes.
  3. Step 3 Place flour, salt and baking soda in a large bowl. Mix eggs and yoghurt together and add to flour with carrot, corn and pumpkin seeds. Do not overmix. Spoon mixture among cases and cook for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown and firm to touch.
