Recipes » Snacks and Sandwiches Oats, coconut and sultana cookies

Ingredients

100 g butter
1/2 cup brown sugar soft , or honey
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg lightly beaten
1 cup flour
1/2 cup oats
2 Tbsp lsa
1 Tbsp milk
1/4 cup coconut desiccated
1/4 cup sultanas

Instructions

  1. Step 1 In a large bowl combine spread, sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy. Add egg, flour, oats, LSA and milk. Mix well then fold in remaining ingredients.
  2. Step 2 Shape into a ball and place on a large square of plastic wrap. Roll into a sausage shape and wrap tightly in plastic wrap.
  3. Step 3 Refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight if possible. When ready to bake, preheat oven to 180ºC and line 2 baking trays with non-stick baking paper.
  4. Step 4 Remove plastic wrap and cut into 15 rounds using a sharp knife. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown. Remove from oven and leave on baking tray for 5 minutes to cool.
  5. Step 5 Remove carefully and place on a wire rack to cool.
