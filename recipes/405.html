Recipes » Main Course Kale with Sausage and White Beans

Ingredients

1 Tbsp olive oil
225 g sausage bulk sweet italian s, or other sausage (use gluten-free sausage if cooking gluten-free)
1.5 cups onion sliced thin
2 cloves garlic sliced thin
450 g kale roughly chopped
1/2 cup chicken stock or vegetable
1 can white beans rinsed and drained
salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. 1 Heat the olive oil in a large sauté pan over medium-high heat and add the bulk sausage; if you can't find bulk sausage, remove the casings on the links. Cook for 3-4 minutes, then add the onion slices and turn the heat to high. Cook until the edges of the onions brown, about 3-4 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. 2 Add the kale, sprinkle salt over everything, then add the chicken stock. Cover the pan, lower the heat to medium and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. 3 Uncover, mix everything well (the kale will have cooked down by now) and add the beans. Cover the pot again and lower the heat to low. Cook another 5 minutes, then turn off the heat. Let stand 5 minutes, then serve.

Prep time 10 • Cook time 20
