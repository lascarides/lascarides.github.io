Recipes » Baking blueberry and bran muffins

Ingredients

1.25 cups self-raising flour
1.5 cups wheat bran
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup blueberries frozen or fresh
2 bananas small, mashed
3/4 cups trim milk
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 egg
2 Tbsp butter melted
1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 190°C. Lightly oil muffin tins.
  2. Step 2 Place sifted flour, bran, salt, cinnamon, sugar, blueberries and mashed banana into a bowl.
  3. Step 3 In a separate bowl or jug, mix together the milk, baking soda, egg, vanilla and melted spread; add to the other ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Step 4 Spoon batter into muffin tins; place in oven and bake for 18-20 minutes or until a skewer inserted comes out clean.
